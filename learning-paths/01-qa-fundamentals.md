# QA Fundamentals

## Who this path is for
- Beginners in QA
- Career switchers
- Anyone confused about where to start

## What you should know after this
- What QA really means
- Difference between testing and checking
- QA mindset and thinking

### 1. ISTQB Foundation Overview
